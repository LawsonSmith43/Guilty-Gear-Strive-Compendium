Unika

Unika originates from a future where an anti-Gear virus developed by Nerville Hammer that Dizzy had become infected with caused all Gears to go berserk, leading to a 'second Crusade'. She hatched from an egg that Dizzy laid before she died, and never knew her birth parents. Despite adopting her, Nerville never saw Unika as a person, and began to use her Gear cells to modify himself. At some point, Nerville and Unika found Axl Low after he had found himself in their future, and Nerville explained the state of their future to him before taking control of him and using his power to send Unika back in time. At some point after this, Nerville sent her to the present from this future, along with the virus, to meet with his present self and explain the circumstances of their future to him. Unika's knowledge of the future aided Nerville in obtaining immense wealth.
