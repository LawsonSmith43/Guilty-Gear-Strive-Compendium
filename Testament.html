Testament

Born in Switzerland[9] during the Crusades, Testament was orphaned at a young age and was taken in by Kliff Undersn, the long-time commander of the Sacred Order of Holy Knights.[7] Deeply grateful,[5] Testament intended to inherit Kliff's name and eventually become his successor.[9] In 2127,[19] in defiance of Kliff's wishes, Testament joined the Order,[19] volunteering despite their hatred for violence, thinking it would make their father happy.[7] Before Testament could make their intentions a reality,[9] in 2128, they met with an untimely end while carrying out a reserve unit's mission on behalf of the Order. Their body was never found.[19]

In truth, Testament was captured by government agents[9] of Nation A.[5] They became the subject of a number of experiments designed to develop new weapons,[15] and transformed into a Gear[9] regardless of their intention[5] by a certain organization.[16] Although Testament managed to remain self-aware after the process,[15] it wasn't long before they turned their fangs toward humanity in accordance with Justice's command of eradicating mankind.[5]

Circa 2140,[note 1] Testament and Kliff met in the battlefield under unknown circumstances, but fighting on opposing sides, Kliff was forced to kill Testament. However, Testament survived the encounter[7]—somehow brought back to life for reasons unknown[9]—and continued to serve Justice, but they and Kliff wouldn't cross paths for the remainder of the war.[7] At some point, Testament presumably met Sol Badguy and learned that he was a Gear.[21]

In 2175, Justice was sealed in a dimensional prison by the Sacred Order, ending the Crusades. While a large majority of Gears became dormant[20] and others gained a will of their own,[22] Testament could not escape her influence. Still forced to comply with her directive, they resolved to free Justice[7] from her weakening seal[19][note 2] to resume her rampage.[21] Unknowingly manipulated by the Conclave,[23][8] Testament worked behind the scenes[11] of the Second Holy Order Selection Tournament organized by the United Nations[20][24] to gather enough energy to revive Justice.[11]
