May

Born to parents who belonged to one of the colonies, May lost her home and entire family to the Crusades at a young age, and wandered the battlefield afterwards. Her original name, birthday or nationality lost to her, all she would ever remember of her childhood is Johnny whisking her away from the savage battlefield in 2174. Johnny took her in, and named her "May" after the month of endless rain, during which she was found. He gave her a new home aboard the May Ship, and the Jellyfish Pirates became her family. She grew to be a integral member of the crew,[16] and was named first mate.

Throughout the long years of living aboard the airship, May developed a fierce love for Johnny: He was part father figure, part brother, and perhaps even part boyfriend to her. However, one day, in 2180, Johnny—who primarily stole from the well-off to aid those in need—was caught in the act and was incarcerated, and was given a long sentence.
