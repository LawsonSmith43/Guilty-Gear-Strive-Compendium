Giovanna

While her past is mostly kept confidential, she spent a certain amount of time living in an old ruined shack with an elderly man she had never previously met, given clothes she never owned after having been picked up off the street from living homeless.

She picked herself up from this situation and became a professional in every aspect, eventually landing a job in the U.S. Government and the Secret Service.

As Goldlewis Dickinson appears to be her boss, he seems to know a great deal about her.
