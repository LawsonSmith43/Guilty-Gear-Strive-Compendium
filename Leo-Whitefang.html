Leo Whitefang

Born sometime during the Crusades, Leo was a gifted combatant since he was a kid, and joined the Sacred Order of Holy Knights in the fight against the Gear menace that threatened to drive mankind to extinction. At some point he met the young prodigy Ky Kiske. Fighting together during the war, they would become friends, though Leo would rather call him an "inspiration" instead. Angry at Ky's friendly attitude and superiority in every regard, Leo cheered himself up by writing down Ky's name next to the entry for "bambino" in his dictionary, but changed his arrogant behavior.

At some point, Leo was assigned command of his own unit. Near the end of the war, humanity's strategic point in the Tasmanian Islands was attacked, endangering the perimeter they maintained. On the Order's behalf, Leo led the charge to secure the border. He and his unit faced what appeared to be certain death, but his leadership allowed them to escape and succeed in their endeavor.[14] The war finally came to an end with Justice's sealing in 2175.

In the following years, Leo and Ky's paths seldom crossed, but then the United Kingdoms of Illyria was formally recognized in 2183. Leo was elected as its Second King to oversee the local government of more than twenty nations, his purview being Old Russia and the Middle Eastern states. Months later, Ky was elected as the First King. At some point, Leo was responsible for brokering an alliance with the Federation of China.
