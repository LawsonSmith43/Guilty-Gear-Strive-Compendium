Asuka R#

An artificial human created by Asuka R. Kreutz, also known as "That Man."

Their biological properties are essentially the same, and he retains Asuka R. Kreutz's memories. However, his somewhat elevated sense of curiosity leads him to a different mindset than the original Asuka R. Kreutz.

His scientific knowledge and technical skills are comparable to the original, meaning he would be capable of creating new artificial humans himself. The third subject Asuka encounters in the simulation, however, has no physical form and only exists within the simulation.

Currently, he is gathering experimental data on Earth for Asuka R. Kreutz's new research he is conducting from outer space, the Battle Method Turing test.
