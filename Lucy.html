Lucy

Lucy, short for Lucyna Kushinada, is a guest character in the Guilty Gear series and is the first ever guest character in the Guilty Gear series, who appears in Guilty Gear -Strive-. Hailing from the animation series adaption of CD Projekt's Cyberpunk series, Cyberpunk: Edgerunners, she is a mysterious netrunner who guides the protagonist of the Cyberpunk: Edgerunners series, David Martinez, into the underworld of Night City. She considers the city a prison and dreams of escaping to the moon.
