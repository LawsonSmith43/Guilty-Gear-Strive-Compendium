Goldlewis Dickinson

Little is known about his past. Born after his older brother, Goldlewis' career took on a different path. During the Crusades, he joined the military to fight the Gear menace and met Vernon E. Groubitz, becoming close friends with him.

Unlike Vernon, however, Goldlewis remained in the service after the war ended, and would become the first active-duty soldier to serve as the Secretary of Defense in U.S. history, taking the position during the presidency of Erica Bartholomew. Also becoming friends with her, he has become a "mainstay of the nation" remaining in his post after Vernon took over from Erica.

Thought the details are unknown, at some point he became estranged from his family due to hiding his "true feelings" from them, believing it was for the best.
